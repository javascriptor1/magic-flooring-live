Coronavirus Announcement

Dear Customers ,

At Magic Flooring, we care about your safety. The following is a quick summary of the procedures that we follow while carrying out installation work on your sites (both residential and commercial): :

  • Wear gloves and masks to all members of the on-site installation team.
  • Sterilization of the goods and the installation kit before entering the customer's site.
  • Sterilizers are always available with the installation team and used when necessary.
  • Clean the site completely before departure and ensure that no items or remainings are left on the site.

For the sake of your safety, we hope that you remain outside the installation place until all the flooring work has been completed

We thank you for your understanding and cooperation and always look forward to serving you in the future.
