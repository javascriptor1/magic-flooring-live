2008

The birth

Our company was created in 2008 under the name "Tawifq Trading Est". Motivated by the booming markets of laminate flooring in Saudi Arabia , we launched our brand of flooring "Xmagic" with a wide range of flooring skirting and accessories to satisfy growing demand in local markets.

2010

Partnership with CLASSEN

We continued to expand our products range by starting a new partnership with one of best laminate flooring manufacturers groups in the world,CLASSEN,Germany. A new distribution outlets created and customers base started to grow on regular basis

2012

New identity and expansion

We had a new indentity. Our company name changed to Magic Flooring and new strategies were formed and pursued for expansion. We have doubled our storage areas and opened new showroom

2014

Big expansion underway

Our customers base exceeded 400 partners and stock level were tripled along with storage areas. Second showroom opened in Dammam.

2019

New partnership with REZULT,Ukraine

A new partnership with REZULT,Ukraine was made and our company start to distribute REZULT PRO flooring in eastern province,Saudi Arabia. This new achievement coupled with an introduction of completely new designs and color for skirting and other flooring accessories which exceeds 60 colors.
